IgniForm is a company focused on developing smart protection technologies and monitoring systems that improve safety in homes, schools, and communities. Guided by the principles of adaptability and resilience, symbolized by the salamander and fire, our mission is to bridge the safety and technology gap in the Philippines by creating accessible IoT and monitoring systems for everyday safety.

From the beginning, the company was organized around collaboration. The operations team managed projects and coordinated activities, while the technology division architects designed frameworks for risk detection and scalability, and information officers and engineers maintained secure platforms. At the same time, safety officers and researchers strengthened both physical and digital protection.

This foundation allowed IgniForm to grow from a student-led initiative into a formal venture. Guided by its mission to provide accessible safety technologies and its vision of building a nationwide smart protection network by 2050, IgniForm continues to foster innovation by opening opportunities for students, tech enthusiasts, and small developers to collaborate.
